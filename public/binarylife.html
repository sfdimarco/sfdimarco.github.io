Base 2: Game of Life

In computer language (Binary), 0 means OFF and 1 means ON.
Click the grid to flip switches, then press Play!

The Rules

  • Lonely: A 1 with fewer than 2 neighbors becomes 0.
  • Happy: A 1 with 2 or 3 neighbors stays 1.
  • Crowded: A 1 with more than 3 neighbors becomes 0.
  • New Friend: A 0 with exactly 3 neighbors becomes 1!
